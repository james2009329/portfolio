Dragster

Materials

  • Steel axles
  • Graphite lubricant
  • Main body printed in white glass fiber impregnated PETG
  • Wheels printed in black glass fiber impregnated PETG
  • Clear spray paint

Design Rationale

Maximized aerodynamic efficiency by minimizing drag. Front wheels recessed, rear tapered, fully enclosed wheels. Internal ribs and hole modifications increased stiffness and minimized friction. Vortex generators tested but not needed due to effective flow separation.
